About Us

Building Youth Leaders Through Adventure & Service

The Lebanese Scout Association (LSA), established in 1935 at the American University of Beirut, is a non-religious, non-sectarian, volunteer-based organization. We are dedicated to educating young people through a value system based on the Scout Promise and Law, aiming to develop responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Read More

Outdoor Education

We provide outdoor and non-formal education programs that foster personal growth and environmental stewardship among youth.

Community Engagement

Our scouts actively participate in community service, promoting civic responsibility and social cohesion across Lebanon.

Leadership Development

Through structured programs, we cultivate leadership skills, empowering youth to take initiative and lead with integrity.

Global Citizenship

As a member of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, we encourage global awareness and cross-cultural understanding.

Keeping scouting alive and connected — wherever we are.

Leader Dashboards

Online Activities

Interactive and educational activities scouts can complete at home or online.

Weekly Tasks
